# integer programming formulation\begin{align}\min \quad & \sum_{i \in V} \sum_{j \in V \setminus \{i\}} c_{ij} x_{ij}  \\\text{s.t.} \quad & \sum_{i \in V_p} \sum_{j \in V \setminus V_p} x_{ij} = 1, \quad \forall p = 1, \ldots, k  \\& \sum_{j \in V_p} \sum_{i \in V \setminus V_p} x_{ij} = 1, \quad \forall p = 1, \ldots, k  \\& \sum_{j \in V \setminus \{i\}} x_{ji} - \sum_{j \in V \setminus \{i\}} x_{ij} = 0, \quad \forall i \in V  \\& x_{ij} \in \{0, 1\}, \quad \forall (i, j) \in A  \\&\begin{aligned}      & u_p - u_q + k \sum_{i \in V_p} \sum_{j \in V_q} x_{ij} \\    & + (k-2) \sum_{i \in V_q} \sum_{j \in V_p} x_{ij} \leq k-1, \\   & \quad p \neq q; \, p, q = 2, \ldots, k  \end{aligned}  \\& u_p \geq 0, \quad p = 2, \ldots, k \end{align}\begin{itemize}    \item \textbf{Objective Function (1):} Minimize the total travel cost.    \item \textbf{Constraint (2):} Each cluster must have exactly one outgoing edge to a node outside the cluster.    \item \textbf{Constraint (3):} Each cluster must have exactly one incoming edge from a node outside the cluster.    \item \textbf{Constraint (4):} Flow conservation constraint to maintain the tour.    \item \textbf{Constraint (5):} Binary variable constraint.    \item \textbf{Constraint (6):} Subtour elimination constraints to prevent subtours.    \item \textbf{Constraint (7):} Non-negativity constraint for the continuous variables used in the subtour elimination constraints.\end{itemize}
