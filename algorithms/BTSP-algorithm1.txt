#integer programming formulation\[\begin{aligned}& \min && \max_{i, j} ~c_{ij} x_{ij} \\& \text{s.t.} && \sum_{j=1, j \ne i}^{n} x_{ij} = 1 \quad \forall i = 1, \ldots, n \\& && \sum_{i=1, i \ne j}^{n} x_{ij} = 1 \quad \forall j = 1, \ldots, n \\& && \sum_{i, j \in S} x_{ij} \leq |S| - 1 \quad \forall S \subset \{1, \ldots, n\}, \ 2 \leq |S| \leq n - 1 \\& && x_{ij} \in \{0, 1\} \quad \forall i, j = 1, \ldots, n\end{aligned}\]
