- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its journey at the depot city (index 0).
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly one city from each of the 7 specified city groups.
- [Requirement 3] Distances between cities (including from and to the depot city) are calculated using Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The objective is to find the shortest possible tour that satisfies the above conditions.
