- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly one city from each of the three city groups: Group 0, Group 1, and Group 2.
- [Requirement 3] Travel cost between two cities is calculated as the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The output should include both the tour as a list of city indices and the total travel cost of the tour.
