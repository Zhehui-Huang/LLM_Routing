- [Requirement 1] The robot starts its journey at the depot city indexed as 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly 8 cities, including the starting depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The tour should both start and end at the depot city.
- [Requirement 4] The path found should be the shortest possible tour that visits exactly 8 cities.
- [Requirement 5] Travel cost between any two cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 6] The output must include the sequence of cities visited in the tour and the total travel cost of that tour.
