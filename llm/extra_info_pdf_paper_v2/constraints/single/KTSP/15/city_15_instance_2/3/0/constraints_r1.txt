- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The tour must include exactly 8 out of the 15 cities.
- [Requirement 3] The goal is to find the shortest possible distance for the robot's travel route.
- [Requirement 4] Travel costs between any two cities are calculated using the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 5] The output must be a list of city indices beginning and ending with the depot city, followed by the total travel cost.
