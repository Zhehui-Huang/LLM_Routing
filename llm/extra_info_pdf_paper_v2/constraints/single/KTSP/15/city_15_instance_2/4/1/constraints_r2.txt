- [Requirement 1]: The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 2]: The robot must visit exactly 8 cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3]: The objective is to minimize the total travel cost, calculated as the Euclidean distance between visited cities.
- [Requirement 4]: The output must include the final tour as a list of city indices and the total travel cost of the tour.
- [Requirement 5]: The tour should include city 0 both as the starting and the ending point.
