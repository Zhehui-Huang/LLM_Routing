- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its journey at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot has to visit exactly 10 cities from a total of 15 cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The tour's travel cost should be calculated as the Euclidean distance between consecutive cities in the tour.
- [Requirement 4] The goal is to find the shortest possible tour that adheres to the above constraints.
- [Requirement 5] Output the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at city 0.
- [Requirement 6] Output the total travel cost of the tour.
