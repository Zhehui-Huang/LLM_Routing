- [Requirement 1] The robot starts and ends its tour at the depot city (city index 0).
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly 4 cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The goal is to find the shortest possible tour that visits these cities.
- [Requirement 4] Travel cost is calculated based on the Euclidean distance between cities.
- [Requirement 5] Output format must include the tour as a list of city indices and the total travel cost of the tour.
- [Requirement 6] Use and adapt strategies from General Variable Neighborhood Search (GVNS) as hinted in the insights provided.
