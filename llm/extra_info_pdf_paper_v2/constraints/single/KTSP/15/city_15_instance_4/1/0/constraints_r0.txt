- [Requirement 1] The robot starts and ends at the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly 12 cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The objective is to find the shortest possible tour.
- [Requirement 4] Travel cost between the cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 5] The solution must represent the tour as a list of city indices, beginning and ending at city 0.
- [Requirement 6] The solution must also output the total travel cost of the tour.
