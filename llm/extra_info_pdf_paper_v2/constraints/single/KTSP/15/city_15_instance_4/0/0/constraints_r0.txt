- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot needs to visit exactly 12 cities in total, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The objective is to find the shortest possible tour covering these 12 cities.
- [Requirement 4] Travel costs between any two cities are calculated based on the Euclidean distance.
