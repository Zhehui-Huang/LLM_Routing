- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its tour at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly 12 out of the 15 given cities.
- [Requirement 3] The objective is to find the shortest possible tour in terms of Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The solution output must include the tour as a list of city indices and the total travel cost.
