- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its journey at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot should visit exactly 13 cities out of the total 20 available cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The measure of travel cost between any two cities is their Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The goal is to minimize the total travel distance for the selected tour.
- [Requirement 5] The output should be presented as a list of city indices representing the tour, starting and ending at city 0, followed by the total travel cost of this tour.
