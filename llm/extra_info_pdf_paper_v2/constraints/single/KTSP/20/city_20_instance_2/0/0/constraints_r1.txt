- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its journey at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot is required to visit exactly 10 cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] Travel costs between cities are determined by the Euclidean distance between them.
- [Requirement 4] The goal is to find the shortest possible tour that meets the above criteria.
- [Requirement 5] Output should include the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending with the depot city.
- [Requirement 6] Output should include the total travel cost of the tour.
