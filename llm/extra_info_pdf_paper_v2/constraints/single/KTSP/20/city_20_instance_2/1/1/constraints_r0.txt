- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its tour at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The tour must exactly visit 10 out of the 20 given cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The goal is to find the shortest possible tour.
- [Requirement 4] The travel cost between cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 5] Output should be provided in the format: a list of city indices for the tour starting and ending at the depot, and the total travel cost of the tour.
