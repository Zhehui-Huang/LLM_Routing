- [Requirement 1] The robot must visit exactly 7 cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 2] The tour must start and end at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 3] The route must minimize the total Euclidean travel distance.
- [Requirement 4] Travel cost between two cities is calculated as the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 5] The output should be in the format of a list of city indices, starting and ending at city 0, followed by the total travel cost.
