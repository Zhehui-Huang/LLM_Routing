- [Requirement 1] The route must start and end at the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly 16 cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between two cities.
- [Requirement 4] The objective is to find the shortest possible tour that includes 16 cities.
- [Requirement 5] The output must include the tour as a list of city indices and the total travel cost of the tour.
