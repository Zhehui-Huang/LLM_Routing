- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its journey at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly 4 cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between any two cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The goal is to find the shortest possible tour that meets the above requirements.
- [Requirement 5] The output must be in the specified format: a list of city indices representing the tour and the total travel cost.
