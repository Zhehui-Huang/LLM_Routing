- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its journey at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] Each city must be visited exactly once by the robot.
- [Requirement 3] The goal is to minimize the longest distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
- [Requirement 4] Travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between cities.
- [Requirement 5] The tour's performance must be measured by calculating both the total travel cost and the maximum distance between any two consecutive cities.
