- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit each city exactly once, excluding the depot which is visited twice (start and end).
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between cities is calculated as the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The goal is to minimize the longest distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
