- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] Each city needs to be visited exactly once by the robot.
- [Requirement 3] The robot's tour should aim to minimize the longest distance between any two consecutive cities.
- [Requirement 4] The robot can travel between any two cities and the travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between those two cities.
- [Requirement 5] Output should include the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 6] Output should include the total travel cost of the tour.
- [Requirement 7] Output should include the maximum distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
