- [Requirement 1] The robot must start at the depot city 0, visit each of the other 14 cities exactly once, and return to the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The goal is to minimize the longest distance between any two consecutive cities in the robot's tour.
- [Requirement 3] Output the tour as a list of city indices, starting and ending at city 0.
- [Requirement 4] Output the total travel cost of the tour.
- [Requirement 5] Output the maximum distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
