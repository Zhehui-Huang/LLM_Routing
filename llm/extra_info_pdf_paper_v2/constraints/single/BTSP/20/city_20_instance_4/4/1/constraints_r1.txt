- [Requirement 1] The robot must visit each city exactly once, starting and ending at the depot city (City 0).
- [Requirement 2] The objective is to minimize the longest distance between any two consecutive cities in the robot's tour.
- [Requirement 3] Output must include the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at depot city 0, the total travel cost of the tour, and the maximum distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
