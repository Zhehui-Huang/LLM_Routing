- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit each city exactly once, except for the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] Travel cost between any two cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] Output should include the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at city 0.
- [Requirement 5] Output must also include the total travel cost of the tour.
