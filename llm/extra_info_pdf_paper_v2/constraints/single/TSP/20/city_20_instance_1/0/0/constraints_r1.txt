- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city (index 0).
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit each of the 19 other cities exactly once during the tour.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost is calculated using the Euclidean distance between the cities.
- [Requirement 4] Output must include the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 5] Output must include the total travel cost of the tour.
