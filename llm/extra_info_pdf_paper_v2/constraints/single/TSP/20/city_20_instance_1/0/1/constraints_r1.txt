- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit each city exactly once, excluding revisits to the depot until the end of the tour.
- [Requirement 3] Travel cost between any two cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] Output the tour as a list of city indices, starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 5] Output the total travel cost of the tour.
