- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end at the depot city, city index 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit all cities exactly once, with the exception of the depot which it returns to at the end.
- [Requirement 3] Travel cost between two cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance formula.
- [Requirement 4] The solution aims to find the shortest tour that satisfies all other constraints.
- [Requirement 5] The output of the solution must be a list of city indices, starting and ending at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 6] The output must also include the total travel cost of the tour.
