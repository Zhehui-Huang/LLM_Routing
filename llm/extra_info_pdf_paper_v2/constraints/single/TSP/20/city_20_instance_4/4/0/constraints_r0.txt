- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city (index 0).
- [Requirement 2] Each city among the 20 cities must be visited exactly once by the robot, except for the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between any two cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The solution must output the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 5] The solution must output the total travel cost of the tour.
