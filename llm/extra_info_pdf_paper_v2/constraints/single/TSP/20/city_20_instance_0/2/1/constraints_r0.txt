- [Requirement 1] The robot must start at the depot city (city 0) and conclude its tour at the same city.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit each city exactly once, except for the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between any two cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance formula.
- [Requirement 4] Output the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 5] Output the total travel cost of the tour.
