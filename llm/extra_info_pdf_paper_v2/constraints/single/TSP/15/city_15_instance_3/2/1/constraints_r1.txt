- [The task is a variant of the Traveling Salesman Problem (TSP) with 15 cities.]
- [The robot must start and end its tour at the depot city 0.]
- [The robot must visit each city exactly once, apart from the depot city.]
- [Travel cost between any two cities is determined by the Euclidean distance.]
- [The output must include the shortest tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at city 0.]
- [The output must also include the total travel cost of the tour.]
