- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit each city exactly once, except for the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between two cities is calculated as the Euclidean distance between them.
- [Requirement 4] The solution should output the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at city 0.
- [Requirement 5] The solution should also output the total travel cost of the tour.
