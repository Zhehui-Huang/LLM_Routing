- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its journey at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit each city (except the depot) exactly once.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between any two cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The solution must provide both the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at the depot city and the total travel cost of this tour.
- [Requirement 5] The robot can travel between any two cities.
- [Requirement 6] The number of cities, including the depot, is 15.
