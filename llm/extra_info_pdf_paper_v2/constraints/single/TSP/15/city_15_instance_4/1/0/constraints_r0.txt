- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its route at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] All cities must be visited exactly once, except the depot city which is visited twice (at the start and end).
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between any two cities is measured as the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The tour should be optimized to find the shortest possible route that meets the previously listed requirements.
- [Requirement 5] The output should format the shortest tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at city 0.
- [Requirement 6] The output should also include the total travel cost of the tour.
