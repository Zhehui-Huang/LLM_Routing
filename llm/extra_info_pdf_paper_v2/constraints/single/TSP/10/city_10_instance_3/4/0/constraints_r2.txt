- [Requirement 1] The robot starts at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit all other cities exactly once and then return to the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between two cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The output should include the shortest possible tour starting and ending at the depot city, and the total travel cost of that tour.
- [Requirement 5] The output should be formatted as a list of city indices for the tour and the total travel cost.
