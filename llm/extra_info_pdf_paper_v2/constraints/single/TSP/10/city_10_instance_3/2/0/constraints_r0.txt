- [Requirement 1] The solution must start and end at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] Each city must be visited exactly once by the robot, excluding the depot city which is visited twice (at the start and at the end).
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between cities is determined by their Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The objective is to determine the shortest possible tour for the robot.
