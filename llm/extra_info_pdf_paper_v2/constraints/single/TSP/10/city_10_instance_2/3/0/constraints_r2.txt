- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] All cities, from city 1 to city 9, must be visited exactly once by the robot.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between any two cities is calculated as the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The robot needs to find the shortest tour that satisfies all other constraints.
- [Requirement 5] The output must include the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 6] The output must also include the total travel cost of the tour.
