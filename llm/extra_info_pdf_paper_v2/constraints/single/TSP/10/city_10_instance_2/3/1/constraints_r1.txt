- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and finish its tour at the depot city, which is City 0.
- [Requirement 2] Each city from 1 to 9 must be visited exactly once in the tour.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between any two cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The goal is to find the shortest tour, i.e., minimizes the total travel distance.
- [Requirement 5] Output the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at City 0.
- [Requirement 6] Output the total travel cost of the tour.
