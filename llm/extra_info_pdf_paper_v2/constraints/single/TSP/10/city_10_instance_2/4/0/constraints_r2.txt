- [Requirement 1] There are 10 cities including the depot. The robot starts and ends the journey at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit all cities exactly once, except the depot.
- [Requirement 3] The robot can travel between any two cities and the travel cost between them is calculated as the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] Output must include the tour starting and ending at the depot city 0, formatted as a list of city indices.
- [Requirement 5] Output must also include the total travel cost of that tour.
