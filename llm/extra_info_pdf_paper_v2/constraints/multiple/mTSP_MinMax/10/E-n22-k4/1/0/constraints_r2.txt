- [Requirement 1] There are 22 cities including a depot city, and each city should be visited exactly once by the robots, except the depot.
- [Requirement 2] All robots start and end their journey at the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] Calculate travel cost as the Euclidean distance between two cities.
- [Requirement 4] The primary objective is to minimize the maximum distance traveled by any of the robots.
- [Requirement 5] The output should include the tour for each robot, starting and ending at the depot, along with the total travel cost for each tour and the maximum travel cost among all tours.
