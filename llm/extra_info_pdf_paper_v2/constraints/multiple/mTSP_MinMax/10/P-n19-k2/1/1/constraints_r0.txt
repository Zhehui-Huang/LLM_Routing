- [Requirement 1] Every city, except the depot, must be visited exactly once by the robots collectively.
- [Requirement 2] Each robot must start and end its tour at the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The primary objective is to minimize the maximum distance traveled by any single robot.
- [Requirement 4] The travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between two cities.
- [Requirement 5] Output should contain the tours for each robot starting and ending at the depot city, the travel cost for each tour, and the maximum travel cost among all tours.
