- [Requirement 5] Objective is to minimize the maximum distance traveled by any single robot.
- [Requirement 6] The output should include the tours for each robot and their corresponding total travel costs, formatted to start and end at the depot city.
- [Requirement 7] You must also output the maximum travel cost among all tours.
