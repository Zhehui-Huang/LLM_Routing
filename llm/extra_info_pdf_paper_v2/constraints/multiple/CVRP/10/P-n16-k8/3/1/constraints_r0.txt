- [Requirement 1] Each robot must start and end its tour at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The total demand of all cities on a robot's tour must not exceed the robot's carrying capacity of 35 units.
- [Requirement 3] The objective is to minimize the total travel cost for all tours collectively.
- [Requirement 4] Each city's specific demand must be fully met by the sum of the deliveries from the robots.
- [Requirement 5] Every city (except the depot) should be visited exactly once by any of the robots.
