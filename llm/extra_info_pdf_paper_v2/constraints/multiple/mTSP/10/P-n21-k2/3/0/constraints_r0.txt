- [Requirement 1] There are 21 cities including the depot city. Cities are indexed starting from 0 up to 20.
- [Requirement 2] The depot city is city 0 with coordinates (30, 40).
- [Requirement 3] Number of robots: 2, all robots start and end their tour at the depot city.
- [Requirement 4] Each robot can travel between any two cities.
- [Requirement 5] Travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between two cities.
- [Requirement 6] Each city, except for the depot, must be visited exactly once by any of the robots.
- [Requirement 7] The objective is to determine the shortest possible tours for the robots while minimizing the total travel cost.
- [Requirement 8] Output the tours for each robot as a list of city indices. Each tour should start and end at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 9] Output the total travel cost for each tour and the overall total travel cost.
