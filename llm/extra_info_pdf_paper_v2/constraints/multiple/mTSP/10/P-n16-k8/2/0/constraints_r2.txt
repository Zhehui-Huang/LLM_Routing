- [Requirement 1] Solve a variant of the TSP/VRP where 8 robots must collectively visit 15 cities once, starting and ending at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] Each city, except the depot city, must be visited exactly once.
- [Requirement 3] Minimize the total travel cost for all tours combined.
- [Requirement 4] Calculate the travel cost as the Euclidean distance between cities.
- [Requirement 5] Output should list each robot's tour with start and end at the depot city, individual travel costs for each tour, and the overall total travel cost.
