- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] Each city must be visited exactly once by the robot.
- [Requirement 3] Minimize the longest distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
- [Requirement 4] The tour shall be output as a list of city indices, starting and ending at depot city 0.
- [Requirement 5] Output the total travel cost of the tour.
- [Requirement 6] Output the maximum distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
- [Requirement 7] Travel cost is calculated using the Euclidean distance between any two cities.
