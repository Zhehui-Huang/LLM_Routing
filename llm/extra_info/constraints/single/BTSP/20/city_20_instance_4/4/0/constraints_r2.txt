- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its journey at the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 2] Each city (from city 1 to city 19) must be visited exactly once by the robot.
- [Requirement 3] Travel costs are calculated using the Euclidean distance between cities.
- [Requirement 4] The goal is to minimize the maximum distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
- [Requirement 5] Output the travel tour as a list of city indices.
- [Requirement 6] Output the total travel cost of the tour.
- [Another Requirement 7] Output the maximum distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
