- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 2] Each city must be visited exactly once by the robot.
- [Requirement 3] The robot's travel between cities is quantified using Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The objective is to minimize the longest distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
- [Requirement 5] Output the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at city 0.
- [Requirement 6] Output the total Euclidean travel cost of the tour.
- [Requirement 7] Output the maximum Euclidean distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
