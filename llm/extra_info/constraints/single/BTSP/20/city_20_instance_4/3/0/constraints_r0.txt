- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city, city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit each city exactly once.
- [Requirement 3] The robot's travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between two consecutive cities.
- [Requirement 4] The objective is to minimize the longest distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
- [Requirement 5] The output of the program should include the complete tour starting and ending at city 0, the total travel cost of the tour, and the maximum distance between any two consecutive cities.
