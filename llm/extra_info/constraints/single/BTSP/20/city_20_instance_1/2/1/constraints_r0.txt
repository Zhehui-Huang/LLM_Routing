- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] Each city must be visited exactly once by the robot.
- [Requirement 3] The primary goal is to minimize the longest distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
- [Requirement 4] The tour should be returned as a list of city indices, starting and ending at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 5] Output the total travel cost of the tour.
- [Requirement 6] Output the maximum distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
- [Requirement 7] Travel cost between two cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance formula.
