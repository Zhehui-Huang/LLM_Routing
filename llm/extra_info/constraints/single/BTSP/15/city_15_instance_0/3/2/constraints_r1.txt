- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 2] Each city must be visited exactly once by the robot, excluding the depot city which is visited twice (start and end).
- [Requirement 3] The goal is to minimize the longest distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
- [Requirement 4] Output the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at city 0.
- [Requirement 5] Output the total travel cost of the tour as the sum of Euclidean distances between consecutively visited cities.
- [Requirement 6] Output the maximum distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
