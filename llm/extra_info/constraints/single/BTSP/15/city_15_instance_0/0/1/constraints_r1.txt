- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the journey at the depot city (City 0).
- [Requirement 2] Each city (1 to 14) must be visited exactly once by the robot.
- [Requirement 3] The objective is to minimize the longest distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
- [Requirement 4] The travel cost between any two cities is calculated as the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 5] The output must include a complete tour starting and ending at the depot city, formatted as a list of city indices.
- [Requirement 6] The output must include the total travel cost of the tour.
- [Requirement 7] The output must include the maximum distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
