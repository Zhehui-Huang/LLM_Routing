- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit each city exactly once.
- [Requirement 3] The goal is to minimize the longest distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
- [Requirement 4] The travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between the two cities.
- [Requirement 5] Output the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at city 0.
- [Requirement 6] Output the total travel cost of the tour.
- [Requirement 7] Output the maximum distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
