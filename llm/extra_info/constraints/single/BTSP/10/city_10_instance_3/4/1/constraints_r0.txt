- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end at the depot city (city 0). 
- [Requirement 2] Each city must be visited exactly once by the robot.
- [Requirement 3] The objective is to minimize the longest distance between any two consecutive cities in the robot's tour. 
- [Requirement 4] The tour output should be a list of city indices starting and ending at city 0.
- [Requirement 5] The output should include the total travel cost of the tour.
- [Requirement 6] The output should include the maximum distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
