- [Requirement 1] The robot starts and ends the tour at depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] Each city must be visited exactly once by the robot.
- [Requirement 3] The objective is to minimize the longest distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
- [Requirement 4] Travel cost between cities is measured using the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 5] The output must include the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at city index 0.
- [Requirement 6] The output must include the total travel cost of the tour.
- [Requirement 7] The output must include the maximum distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
