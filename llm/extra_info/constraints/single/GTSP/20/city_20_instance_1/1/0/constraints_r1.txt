- [Requirement 1] The robot must begin and end its tour at the depot city, which is city index 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly one city from each specified group of cities. There are four total groups.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between cities is calculated based on the Euclidean distance between them.
- [Requirement 4] The solution must identify the shortest possible tour that satisfies the other requirements. 
- [Requirement 5] Output the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at city index 0.
- [Requirement 6] Output the total travel cost of the tour.
