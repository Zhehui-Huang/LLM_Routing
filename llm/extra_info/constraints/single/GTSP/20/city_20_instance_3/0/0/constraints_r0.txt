- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly one city from each of the six city groups.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between any two cities is calculated as their Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The objective is to minimize the total travel cost of the tour.
- [Requirement 5] Output should include the tour sequence starting and ending at city 0, and the corresponding total travel cost.
