- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its tour at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly one city from each of the 6 city groups and only one city from each group.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between two cities is calculated as the Euclidean distance between them.
- [Requirement 4] Output the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 5] Output the total travel cost of the tour.
