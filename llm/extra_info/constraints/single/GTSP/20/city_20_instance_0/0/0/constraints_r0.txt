- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its tour at the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly one city from each of the three groups.
- [Requirement 3] The tour's travel cost is calculated using the Euclidean distance between cities.
- [Requirement 4] The output should include the tour as a list of city indices, starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 5] The output should also include the total travel cost of the tour.
