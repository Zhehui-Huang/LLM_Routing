- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its tour at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly one city from each of the five city groups.
- [Requirement 3] The total travel cost, calculated as the Euclidean distance between consecutive cities in the tour, must be minimized.
- [Requirement 4] The output must include the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at city 0.
- [Requirement 5] The output must also include the total travel cost of the tour.
