- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly one city from each of the seven city groups.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between any two cities.
- [Requirement 4] The output must include the tour as a list of city indices, starting and ending at city 0.
- [Requirement 5] The output must include the total travel cost of the tour.
