- [Requirement 1] The robot should start and end the journey at the depot city (City 0).
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly one city from each of the four city groups.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost is computed as the Euclidean distance between two cities.
- [Requirement 4] Find the shortest possible route that satisfies the above requirements.
- [Requirement 5] The output should include the tour as a list of city indices and the total travel cost. The list should start and end with city 0.
