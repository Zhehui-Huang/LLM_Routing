- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly one city from each city group (four groups total).
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between cities is calculated based on the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The objective is to find the shortest possible tour meeting the above requirements.
- [Requirement 5] Output the tour as a list of city indices and the total travel cost.
