- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly one city from each of the four groups.
- [Requirement 3] The robot can travel between any two cities and the travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between these cities.
- [Requirement 4] The outcome must include the shortest tour and total travel cost, formatted as a list of city indices starting and ending at city 0, and the numeric total travel cost.
