- [Requirement 1] The robot starts and ends its tour at the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly one city from each of the four city groups.
- [Requirement 3] Travel cost between cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] Output must include the tour as a list of city indices, starting and ending at city 0.
- [Requirement 5] Output must include the total travel cost of the tour.
