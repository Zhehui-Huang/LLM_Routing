- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its tour at the depot city, which is city index 0.
- [Requirement 2] The tour must include exactly 10 distinct cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The objective is to minimize the total travel cost, calculated as the sum of the Euclidean distances between consecutively visited cities.
- [Requirement 4] The robot can travel directly between any two cities.
- [Requirement 5] Output should include the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at the depot, and the total travel cost of this tour.
