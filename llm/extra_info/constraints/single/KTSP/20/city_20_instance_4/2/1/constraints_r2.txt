- [Requirement 1] The robot must visit exactly 16 cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 2] The robot's tour must start and end at the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between any two cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] Find the shortest possible tour that meets the above conditions.
- [Requirement 5] Output the tour as a list of city indices.
- [Requirement 6] Output the total travel cost of the tour.
