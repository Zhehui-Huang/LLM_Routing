- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its tour at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot needs to visit exactly 16 different cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The solution should find the shortest possible route that satisfies the above conditions.
- [Requirement 4] Travel cost between cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance formula.
- [Requirement 5] The output should list the tour as a sequence of city indices.
- [Requirement 6] The output should also provide the total travel cost of the tour.
