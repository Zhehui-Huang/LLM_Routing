- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly 13 cities out of the total 20, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The path of the robot should be the shortest possible tour among the chosen cities.
- [Requirement 4] The travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between cities.
- [Requirement 5] The output should include the sequence of the tour as a list of city indices and the total travel cost.
