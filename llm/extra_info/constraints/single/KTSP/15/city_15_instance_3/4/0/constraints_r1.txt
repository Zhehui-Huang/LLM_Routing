- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly 10 cities out of the 15 available, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] Travel cost between two cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The objective is to find the shortest possible tour that fulfills the aforementioned conditions.
