- [Requirement 1] There are 15 cities including the depot city.
- [Requirement 2] The robot starts and ends its route at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 3] The robot must visit exactly 6 cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 4] The travel cost between any two cities is calculated as the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 5] The goal is to find the shortest possible tour that involves visiting exactly 6 cities.
- [Requirement 6] The tour output should be a list of city indices, starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 7] The output should also include the total travel cost of the tour.
