- [Requirement 1] The tour must start and end at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly 7 different cities, including the depot.
- [Requirement 3] Travel cost between cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The goal is to find the shortest possible tour covering 7 cities.
- [Requirement 5] Output the tour as a list of city indices, beginning and concluding at city 0.
- [Requirement 6] Output the total travel cost of the tour.
