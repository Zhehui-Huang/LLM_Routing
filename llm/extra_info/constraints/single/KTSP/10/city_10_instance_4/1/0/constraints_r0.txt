- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot needs to visit exactly 8 cities from the set of 10 available cities.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost is calculated based on the Euclidean distance between each pair of cities.
- [Requirement 4] The solution should identify the shortest possible tour that completes the above requirements.
- [Requirement 5] The solution should output the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 6] The solution should also output the total travel cost of the tour.
