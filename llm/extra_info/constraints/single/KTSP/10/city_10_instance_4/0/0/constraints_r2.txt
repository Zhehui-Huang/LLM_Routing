- [Requirement 1] The robot will start and end its tour at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly 8 out of the 10 available cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between any two cities is calculated based on the Euclidean distance between their coordinates.
- [Requirement 4] The goal is to find the shortest possible tour that satisfies the above constraints.
