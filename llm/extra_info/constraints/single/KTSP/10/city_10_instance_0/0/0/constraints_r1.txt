- [Requirement 1] There are 10 cities, named from 0 to 9, with their respective coordinates provided.
- [Requirement 2] The robot starts at the depot city (city 0) and must visit exactly 4 cities including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The tour must begin and end at the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 4] The itinerary is to consist of exactly 5 segments: departing from city 0, visiting 3 other cities, and returning to city 0.
- [Requirement 5] The travel cost is calculated using the Euclidean distance between successive city pairs in the tour.
- [Requirement 6] The goal is to find the shortest possible tour based on the total travel cost.
