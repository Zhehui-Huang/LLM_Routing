- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its journey at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly four cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The objective is to find the minimum distance route that satisfies the above conditions.
- [Requirement 4] The travel distance between any two cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance formula.
- [Requirement 5] The final output should include the tour as a list of city indices and the total travel cost of that tour.
