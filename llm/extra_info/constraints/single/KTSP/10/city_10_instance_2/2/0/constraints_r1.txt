- [Requirement 1] The tour must start and end at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly 6 cities (including the depot city).
- [Requirement 3] The goal is to find the shortest possible tour that satisfies the previous conditions.
- [Requirement 4] The travel cost between cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance formula.
- [Requirement 5] The output should list the tour as a series of city indices and the total travel cost.
