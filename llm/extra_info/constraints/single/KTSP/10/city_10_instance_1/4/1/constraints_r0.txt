- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city (City 0).
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly 5 cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The robot can travel between any two cities.
- [Requirement 4] The travel cost between any two cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 5] The output should list the indices of the cities in the order they are visited, starting and ending with the depot city.
- [Requirement 6] The output should also include the total travel cost of the tour.
