- [Requirement 1] The tour must start and end at depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly 5 cities, including the depot.
- [Requirement 3] Each city can be visited only once in the tour, except for the depot city which is visited twice (starting and ending point).
- [Requirement 4] The travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between two cities.
- [Requirement 5] The solution should find the shortest possible tour fulfilling the above requirements.
- [Requirement 6] The output should include the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 7] The output should also include the total travel cost of the tour.
