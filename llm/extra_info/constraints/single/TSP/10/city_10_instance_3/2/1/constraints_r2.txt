- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit each of the other cities exactly once.
- [Requirement 3] Travel cost between two cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The solution should provide the shortest possible tour that meets the above conditions.
- [Requirement 5] The output should list the city indices of the tour starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 6] The output should also include the total travel cost of the tour.
