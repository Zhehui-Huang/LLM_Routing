- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit all cities exactly once.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between the cities.
- [Requirement 4] The solution must provide a list of city indices representing the tour, starting and ending at city 0.
- [Requirement 5] The solution must provide the total travel cost of the tour.
