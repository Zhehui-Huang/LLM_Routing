- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the journey at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit all other cities exactly once before returning to the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] Travel cost between cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] Output should present the tour starting and ending at city 0 and a list of the city indices visited in order.
- [Requirement 5] Output should also include the total travel cost of the tour.
