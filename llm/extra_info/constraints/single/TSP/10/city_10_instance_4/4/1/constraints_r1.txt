- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] Each city must be visited exactly once by the robot, except the depot city.
- [OnChange 3] The travel cost between any two cities is determined by the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The solution should find the shortest possible tour visiting all specified cities.
- [Requirement 5] The output should consist of a list of visited city indices in order, starting and ending at city 0, and the total travel cost of the tour.
