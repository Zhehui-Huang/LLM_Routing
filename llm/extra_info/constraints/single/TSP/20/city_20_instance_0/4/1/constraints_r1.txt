- [Requirement 1] There are 20 cities, including the depot city 0. Each city is defined by its coordinates.
- [Requirement 2] The robot can travel between any two cities, with the travel cost calculated as the Euclidean distance between the two cities.
- [Requirement 3] The robot must start its route at the depot city 0, visit all other cities exactly once, and then return back to the depot city 0. The objective is to find the shortest possible tour.
