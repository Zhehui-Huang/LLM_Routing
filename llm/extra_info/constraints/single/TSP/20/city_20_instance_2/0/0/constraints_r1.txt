- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its tour at the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit each city exactly once, except the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] Travel cost between cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The goal is to find the shortest possible tour covering all the required cities.
- [Requirement 5] Output should include the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at city 0.
- [Requirement 6] Output should also include the total travel cost of the tour.
