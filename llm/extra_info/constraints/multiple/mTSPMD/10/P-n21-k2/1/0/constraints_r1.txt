- [Requirement 1] Each robot must start and end its tour at its assigned depot city.
- [Requirement 2] All cities must be visited exactly once by the robots collectively, with each city visited by only one robot.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost for moving between cities is calculated using Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The objective is to minimize the total travel cost for all robots combined.
- [Requirement 5] Output should list the tour for each robot starting and ending at its depot and include the travel cost for each robot's tour as well as the overall total travel cost.
