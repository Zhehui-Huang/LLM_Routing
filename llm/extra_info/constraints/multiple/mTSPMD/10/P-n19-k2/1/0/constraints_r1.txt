- [Requirement 1] Each robot must start and end its journey at its assigned depot city.
- [Requirement 2] Each city must be visited exactly once by exactly one robot.
- [Requirement 3] The objective is to minimize the total combined travel cost of all robot tours.
- [Requirement 4] The tours for each robot should be represented as a list of city indices, starting and ending at the respective depot city.
- [Requirement 5] The output should include the total travel cost for each robot's tour and the overall total travel cost across all tours.
