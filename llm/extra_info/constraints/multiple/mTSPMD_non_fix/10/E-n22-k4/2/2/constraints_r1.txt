- [Requirement 2]: Each robot starts from depot city 0.
- [Requirement 3]: Each robot can end its tour at any city; it does not need to return to the depot.
- [Requirement 4]: Minimize the total cumulative travel cost for all robots.
- [Requirement 5]: Travel cost is the Euclidean distance between cities.
