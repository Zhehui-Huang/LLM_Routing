- [Requirement 3] Tours should be optimized for the lowest total travel distance or cost across all robots.
- [Requirement 4] Travel cost must be calculated as the Euclidean distance between two cities.

- [Requirement 6] Output the tours for each robot as a list of city indices, starting and potentially ending at its assigned depot.
