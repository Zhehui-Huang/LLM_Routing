- [Requirement 1] Robots must start and finish their routes at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] Each city's demand must be completely fulfilled.
- [Requirement 3] The total load carried by any robot should not exceed its capacity limit of 6000.
- [Requirement 4] Minimize the total travel cost across all robot routes.
- [Requirement 5] Each route must be generated keeping in mind the Euclidean distance travel costs between cities.
- [Requirement 6] Deliver goods to all cities, with each city visited at least once by any of the robots.
- [Requirement 7] Provide a list of tours for each robot, indicating the sequence of city visits, starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 8] Provide the total travel cost for each tour and the cumulative travel cost across all tours.
