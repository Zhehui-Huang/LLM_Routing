- [Requirement 3] Minimize the total travel cost for all tours, calculated as the Euclidean distance between two cities.

- [Requirement 5] Ensure the robot carrying capacity of 6000 units is not exceeded during any tour.
- [Requirement 6] Output the tours for each robot as a list of city indices, along with the total travel cost for each tour and the combined total travel cost across all tours.
