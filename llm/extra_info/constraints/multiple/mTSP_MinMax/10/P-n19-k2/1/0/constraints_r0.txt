- [Requirement 1] All cities, except the depot, must be visited exactly once by the robots collectively.
- [Requirement 2] Each robot starts and ends its tour at the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The primary objective is to minimize the maximum distance traveled by any single robot.
- [Requirement 4] The travel cost between two cities is calculated as the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 5] Output the tours for each robot as a list of city indices, starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 6] Output the travel cost for each tour and also the maximum travel cost among all the tours.
