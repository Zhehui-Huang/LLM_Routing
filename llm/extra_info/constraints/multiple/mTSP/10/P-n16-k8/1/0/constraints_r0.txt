- [Requirement 1] All robots must start and end their tour at the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 2] Each robot can visit each city exactly once, except for the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The objective is to minimize the total travel cost for all tours combined.
- [Requirement 4] Output should include individual tours for each robot, each formatted as a list of city indices starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 5] Output should report the total travel cost for each tour and the cumulative travel cost for all tours.
