- [Requirement 1] There are 22 cities including the depot. Each non-depot city must be visited exactly once by any of the robots.
- [Requirement 2] All robots start and end their journey at the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between two cities is calculated as the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The total number of robots available is 4.
- [Requirement 5] The goal is to minimize the total travel cost for all robots.
- [Requirement 6] The output should list the tours for each robot starting and ending at the depot city, along with the total travel cost for each tour and the overall total travel cost.
