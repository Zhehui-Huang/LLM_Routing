- [Requirement 1] There are 19 cities including a depot city, with known coordinates for each city.
- [Requirement 2] There are 2 robots, and both start and end their journey at the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] Robots can travel between any two cities and the travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between two cities.
- [Requirement 4] Each robot must collectively visit all cities exactly once, excluding the depot city as a destination.
- [Requirement 5] Minimize the total travel cost across all robots, which includes minimizing the travel cost for each individual robot's tour.
- [Requirement 6] Each tour must start and end at the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 7] Outputs should list the tour for each robot as a list of city indices starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 8] The output should include the total travel cost for each individual robot's tour and the overall total travel cost for all tours.
