- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot needs to visit exactly one city from each of the three groups. Group 0 includes cities [2, 7, 10, 11, 14], Group 1 includes [1, 3, 5, 8, 13], and Group 2 includes [4, 6, 9, 12].
- [Requirement 3] The aim is to find the shortest tour possible, i.e., to minimize the total Euclidean distance traveled by the robot.
