- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit all cities exactly once, excluding the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The robot can travel between any two cities, and the travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between the two cities.
- [Requirement 4] Output should contain the list of city indices starting and ending at city 0 along with the total travel cost.
- [Requirement 5] Minimize the total travel cost for the tour which visits all cities exactly once and returns to the starting city.
