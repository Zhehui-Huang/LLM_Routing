- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit each city exactly once, except the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The robot can travel between any two cities, and the travel cost between cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The objective is to find the shortest possible tour that satisfies the above constraints.
- [Requirement 5] The output must include the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 6] The output must also include the total travel cost of the tour.
