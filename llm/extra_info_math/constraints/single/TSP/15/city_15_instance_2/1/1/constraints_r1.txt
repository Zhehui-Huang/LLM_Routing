- [Requirement 1] The robot must visit all cities exactly once, except the depot city, and then return to the depot city.
- [Requirement 2] The travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between any two cities.
- [Requirement 3] The tour should start and end at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 4] The solution must output the tour as a list of city indices, starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 5] The solution must output the total travel cost of the tour.
