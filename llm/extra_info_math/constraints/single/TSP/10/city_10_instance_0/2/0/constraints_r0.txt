- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its tour at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit each city exactly once, except for the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] Travel costs between the cities are calculated based on the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] No subtours are allowed; the robot must visit each city only once before returning to the depot.
