- [Requirement 1] The robot must visit all cities exactly once, except for the depot city.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must start and end its journey at the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 3] The total travel distance should be minimized, using the Euclidean distance between cities to calculate the travel cost.
- [Requirement 4] The travel between any two cities is represented by a binary decision (1 if the city is visited directly after another, 0 otherwise).
- [Requirement 5] Each city is to be departed exactly once.
- [Requirement 6] Each city is to be arrived at exactly once.
- [Requirement 7] Subtours involving two or more cities apart from the complete tour must be prevented (subtour elimination).
