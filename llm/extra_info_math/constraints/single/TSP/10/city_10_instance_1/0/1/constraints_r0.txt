- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its tour at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] Each city (including the depot) must be visited exactly once by the robot.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between any two cities. The total travel cost of the tour should be minimized.
- [Requirement 4] The output should be formatted as a list of city indices showing the tour starting and ending at the depot city 0, followed by the total travel cost of the tour.
