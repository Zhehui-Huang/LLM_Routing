- [Requirement 1] The robot needs to start and end at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit all cities exactly once except the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between the cities.
- [Requirement 4] Minimize the total cost of the tour.
- [Requirement 5] For each city \( i \), ensure there is exactly one departure to another city.
- [Requirement 6] For each city \( j \), ensure it is entered exactly once from another city.
- [Requirement 7] No subtours are allowed; this must be enforced through appropriate constraints such as subtour elimination constraints.
