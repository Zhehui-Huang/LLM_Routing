- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city (City 0).
- [Requirement 2] Each city must be visited exactly once by the robot, apart from the depot city which is the start and end point.
- [Requirement 3] Travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
- [Requirement 4] The objective is to minimize the total travel cost for the tour.
- [Requirement 5] The tour must not contain any subtours; all cities should form a single continuous loop returning to the starting city.
