- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit each city exactly once before returning to the depot.
- [Requirement 3] The robot can travel between any two cities and the travel cost is the Euclidean distance between those cities.
- [Requirement 4] The solution must minimize the total travel cost of the tour.
- [Requirement 5] All solutions must prevent subtours, allowing only a single tour visiting all cities exactly once.
