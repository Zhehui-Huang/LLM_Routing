- [Requirement 1] The robot starts at depot city 0 and must return to depot city 0 after visiting all other cities.
- [Requirement 2] Each city (from 1 to 19) must be visited exactly once by the robot.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between any two cities.
- [Requirement 4] The objective is to minimize the total travel distance of the complete tour.
- [Requirement 5] No city is visited more than once in the tour, except for the depot city which is visited exactly twice (start and end of the tour).
