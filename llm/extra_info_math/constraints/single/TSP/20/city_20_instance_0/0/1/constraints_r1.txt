- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its tour at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit each city exactly once, except the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between any two cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The solution must minimize the total travel cost of the tour.
- [Requirement 5] Output the tour as a list of city indices, starting and ending at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 6] Output the total travel cost of the tour.
