- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit all other cities exactly once.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between cities should be calculated using the Euclidean formula.
- [Requirement 4] The tour should minimize the total travel cost.
- [Requirement 5] Output should be formatted as a tour list of city indices, starting and ending at the depot (city 0), and the total travel cost of that tour.
