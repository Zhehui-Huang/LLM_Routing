- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and finish the tour at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] Each city, including the depot city, needs to be visited exactly once.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between two cities.
- [Requirement 4] The objective is to minimize the longest distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
- [Requirement 5] The solution should provide the complete tour specifying the sequence of city indices.
- [Requirement 6] The solution should provide the total travel cost of the tour.
- [Requirement 7] The solution should provide the maximum distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
