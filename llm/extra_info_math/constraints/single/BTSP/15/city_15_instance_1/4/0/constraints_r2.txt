- [Requirement 1]: The robot must visit each city exactly once and return to the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 2]: The travel cost between any two cities is calculated as the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 3]: The objective is to minimize the longest distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
- [Requirement 4]: The output must include the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 5]: The output must include the total travel cost of the tour.
- [Requirement 6]: The output must include the maximum distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
