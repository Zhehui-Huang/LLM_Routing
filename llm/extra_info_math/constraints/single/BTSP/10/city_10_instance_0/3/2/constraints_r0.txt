- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its tour at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] Each city must be visited exactly once by the robot.
- [Requirement 3] The objective is to minimize the longest distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
- [Requirement 4] The distance between any two cities is computed using the Euclidean distance formula.
- [Requirement 5] The robot can travel between any two cities.
- [Requirement 6] The output should include the tour as a list starting and ending at city 0, total travel cost of the tour, and the maximum distance between any two consecutive cities.
