- [Requirement 1] The tour must include exactly 6 cities, starting and ending at the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 2] Each city can only be visited once in the tour, except for the depot city which must be both the start and the end of the tour.
- [Requirement 3] Travel costs are calculated based on the Euclidean distance between cities, and the goal is to minimize the total travel distance for the tour.
