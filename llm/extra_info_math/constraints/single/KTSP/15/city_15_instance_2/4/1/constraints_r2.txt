- [Requirement 1] The robot starts at the depot city 0 and must return to city 0 after completing the tour.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly 8 cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The robot can travel between any two cities with the travel cost calculated as the Euclidean distance between those cities.
- [Requirement 4] The goal is to find the shortest possible tour that covers these 8 cities.
- [Requirement 5] Output should be in the form of a list starting and ending at the depot city, with the total travel cost.
- [Requirement 6] The entire solution must adhere to the constraint formulations provided for the k-TSP.
