- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot needs to visit exactly 16 cities from the given 20 cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The goal is to find the shortest possible tour that visits these 16 cities.
- [Requirement 4] The travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between cities.
