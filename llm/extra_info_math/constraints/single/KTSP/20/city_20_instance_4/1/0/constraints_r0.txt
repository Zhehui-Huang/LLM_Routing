- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its journey at the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly 16 cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The path determined should be the shortest possible route that adheres to the above requirements.
- [Requirement 4] The travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between two cities.
- [Requirement 5] The solution must provide the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 6] The solution must also provide the total travel cost of the tour.
