- [Requirement 1] Robot starts and ends the tour at the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 2] Robot must visit exactly 13 cities from the list of 20 cities available.
- [Requirement 3] Travel cost between any two cities is calculated as the Euclidean distance between them.
- [Requirement 4] The goal is to find the shortest possible tour that adheres to the above constraints.
