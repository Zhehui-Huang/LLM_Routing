- [Requirement 1] There is one robot that starts and ends the tour at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly 10 cities from the given set of 20 cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between any two cities.
- [Requirement 4] The goal is to find the shortest possible tour that visits exactly 10 cities.
- [Requirement 5] Output the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 6] Output the total travel cost of the tour.
