- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its tour at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot needs to visit exactly one city from each of the three city groups:
  - Group 0: [1, 3, 5, 11, 13, 14, 19]
  - Group 1: [2, 6, 7, 8, 12, 15]
  - Group 2: [4, 9, 10, 16, 17, 18]
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between cities is calculated as the Euclidean distance between them.
- [Requirement 4] The output should include the tour as a list of city indices, starting and ending at city 0.
- [Requirement 5] The output should also include the total travel cost of the tour.
