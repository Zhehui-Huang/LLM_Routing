- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city (City 0).
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly one city from each group of cities.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between two cities.
- [Requirement 4] The solution should output the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 5] The solution should output the total travel cost of the tour.
