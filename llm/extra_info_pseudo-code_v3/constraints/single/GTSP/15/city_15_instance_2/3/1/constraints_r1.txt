- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city (City 0).
- [Requirement 2] The tour must include exactly one city from each of the five predefined city groups.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost should be calculated as the Euclidean distance between any two cities.
- [Requirement 4] The output should present the final tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 5] The output should also provide the total travel cost of the tour.
