- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its journey at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly one city from each group of cities.
- [Requirement 3] The robot's travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between any two cities it visits.
- [Requirement 4] The output should include the sequence of city indices forming the shortest tour, starting and ending at city 0.
- [Requirement 5] The output should also include the total travel cost of the shortest tour.
