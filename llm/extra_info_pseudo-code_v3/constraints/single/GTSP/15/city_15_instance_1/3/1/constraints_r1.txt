- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city (City 0).
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly one city from each city group.
- [Requirement 3] The total travel cost is measured as the Euclidean distance between cities.
- [Requirement 4] The objective is to find the shortest possible tour that satisfies the above conditions.
- [Requirement 5] Output the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 6] Output the total travel cost of the tour.
