- [Requirement 1] The robot starts and ends its journey at the depot city (index 0).
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly one city from each group of cities.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between cities is computed using the Euclidean distance formula.
- [Requirement 4] The solution should aim to find the shortest tour possible.
- [Requirement 5] Output should list the city indices in the order visited, starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 6] Output should include the total travel cost of the tour.
