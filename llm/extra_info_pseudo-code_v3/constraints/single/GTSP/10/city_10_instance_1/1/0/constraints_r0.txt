- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its tour at depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot needs to visit exactly one city from each of the four city groups.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between any two cities.
- [Requirement 4] The tour should be represented as a list of city indices, starting and ending at depot city 0.
- [Requirement 5] The output must also include the total travel cost of the tour.
