- [Requirement 1] The robot must visit all cities exactly once, except the depot city.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 3] The return output must include the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at city 0.
- [Requirement 4] The return output must also include the total travel cost of the tour.
- [Requirement 5] The travel cost between two cities is calculated as the Euclidean distance between them.
