- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its tour at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit all cities exactly once, except the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between any two cities is calculated as the Euclidean distance between them.
- [Requirement 4] Use the Christofides algorithm to find the shortest tour for the robot.
- [Requirement 5] Output the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at city 0.
- [Requirement 6] Output the total travel cost of the tour.
