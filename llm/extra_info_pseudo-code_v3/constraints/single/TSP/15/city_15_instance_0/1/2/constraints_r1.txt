- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] Each city, except the depot, must be visited exactly once by the robot.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between any two cities is calculated based on the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The solution must identify the shortest possible tour that fulfills the specified travel conditions.
- [Requirement 5] Output the tour in the format of a list of city indices starting and ending at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 6] Output the total travel cost of the tour.
