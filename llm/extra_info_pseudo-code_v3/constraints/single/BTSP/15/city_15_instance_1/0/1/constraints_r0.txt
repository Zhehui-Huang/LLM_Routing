- [Requirement 2] The goal is to minimize the longest distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
- [Requirement 3] The output should include the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at city 0, the total travel cost of the tour, and the maximum distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
