- [Requirement 1] The robot must visit each city exactly once, start and end at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The goal is to minimize the longest distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
- [Requirement 3] Calculate the total travel cost as the sum of distances between consecutive cities in the tour.
- [Requirement 4] Calculate the Euclidean distance as the travel cost between any two cities.
- [Requirement 5] Output the tour, total travel cost, and maximum distance between consecutive cities.
