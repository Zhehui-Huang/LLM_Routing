- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its journey at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] Each city must be visited exactly once by the robot, aside from the depot city which must be visited twice (as start and end point).
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between any two cities is calculated based on the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The goal is to minimize the longest distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
- [Requirement 5] The output should include the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at city 0.
- [Requirement 6] The output should include the total travel cost of the tour.
- [Requirement 7] The output should include the maximum distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
