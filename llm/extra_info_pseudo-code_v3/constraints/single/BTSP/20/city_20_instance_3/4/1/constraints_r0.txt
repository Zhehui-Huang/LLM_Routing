- [Requirement 1] The robot must visit each city exactly once, starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 2] The goal is to minimize the longest distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between the two cities.
- [Requirement 4] The robot can travel between any two cities.
- [Requirement 5] Output the tour as a list of city indices, starting and ending at the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 6] Output the total travel cost of the tour.
- [Requirement 7] Output the maximum distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
