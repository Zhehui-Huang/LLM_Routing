- [Requirement 1] Robot must visit each city exactly once, starting and ending at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The goal is to minimize the longest distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between any two cities is calculated as the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] Output must include the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at city 0.
- [Requirement 5] Output must include the total travel cost of the tour.
- [Requirement 6] Output must include the maximum distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
