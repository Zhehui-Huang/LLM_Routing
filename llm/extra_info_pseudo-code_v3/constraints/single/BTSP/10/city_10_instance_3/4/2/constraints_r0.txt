- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the journey at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] Each city must be visited exactly once by the robot, following the rules of the Traveling Salesman Problem.
- [Requirement 3] The goal is to minimize the longest distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
- [Requirement 4] The tour should be output as a list of city indices starting and ending at city 0.
- [Requirement 5] The total travel cost of the tour, which is the sum of the distances between consecutive cities, should be output.
- [Requirement 6] The maximum distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour should be output.
- [Requirement 7] The travel cost between any two cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance formula.
