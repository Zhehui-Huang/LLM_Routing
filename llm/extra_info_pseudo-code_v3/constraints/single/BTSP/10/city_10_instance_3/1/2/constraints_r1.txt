- [Requirement 1] The robot starts and ends at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot visits each city exactly once.
- [Requirement 3] The objective is to minimize the longest distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
- [Requirement 4] The travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between two cities.
- [Requirement 5] Output the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at city 0.
- [Requirement 6] Output the total travel cost of the tour.
- [Requirement 7] Output the maximum distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
