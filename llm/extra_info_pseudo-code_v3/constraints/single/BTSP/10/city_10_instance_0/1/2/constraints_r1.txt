- [Requirement 1] The robot must visit each city exactly once, starting and ending at the depot city (City 0).
- [Requirement 2] The goal is to minimize the longest distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
- [Requirement 3] The output should list the tour as a sequence of city indices starting and ending at depot city 0.
- [Requirement 4] The output should also report the total travel cost of the tour.
- [Requirement 5] Additionally, the output must specify the maximum distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
