- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly 10 cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The goal is to find the shortest possible tour that visits these cities.
- [Requirement 4] The travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between two cities.
- [Requirement 5] The tour output should be a list of city indices.
- [Requirement 6] Output should also include the total travel cost of the tour.
