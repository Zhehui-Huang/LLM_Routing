- [Requirement 1] The robot starts at the depot city 0 and ends at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly 6 cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The tour must seek the shortest possible distance.
- [Requirement 4] Travel cost between any two cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 5] Output the tour as a list of city indices, starting and ending at depot city 0.
- [Requirement 6] Output the total travel cost of the tour.
- [Requirement 7] Implement the solution using the General Variable Neighborhood Search (GVNS) algorithm with specified subroutines and neighborhood structures.
