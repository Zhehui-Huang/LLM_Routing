- [Requirement 1]: The robot must start and end its journey at the depot city, identified as city 0.
- [Requirement 2]: The robot must visit exactly 8 cities out of the total 15 provided.
- [Requirement 3]: The objective is to minimize the total Euclidean travel distance of the tour that visits the selected cities.
- [Requirement 4]: The output should include both the tour as a list of city indices, beginning and ending at city 0, and the total travel cost of the tour.
