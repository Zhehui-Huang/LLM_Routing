- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot needs to visit exactly 12 cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The goal is to find the shortest possible tour.
- [Requirement 4] The tour output should be in the form of a list of city indices, starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 5] The output must also include the total travel cost of the tour.
