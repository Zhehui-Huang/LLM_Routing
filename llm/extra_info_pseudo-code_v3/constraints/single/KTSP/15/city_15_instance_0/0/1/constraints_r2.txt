- [Requirement 1] The robot must visit exactly 4 cities, including the depot city, and must start and end the tour at the depot city.
- [Requirement 2] The travel cost between two cities is calculated as the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 3] The goal is to find the shortest possible tour that visits exactly 4 cities.
- [Requirement 4] Output the tour as a list of city indices, starting and ending at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 5] Output the total travel cost of the tour.
