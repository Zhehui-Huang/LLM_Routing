- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The tour must include exactly 5 cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The objective is to find the shortest possible tour.
- [Requirement 4] The solution must return the tour as a list of city indices, starting and ending at city 0.
- [Requirement 5] The solution must also return the total travel cost of the tour.
