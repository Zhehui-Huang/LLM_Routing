- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly 5 cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The robot's travel cost is calculated using the Euclidean distance between two cities.
- [Requirement 4] The goal is to find the shortest possible tour that meets the above conditions.
- [Requirement 5] Output the tour as a list of city indices, starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 6] Output the total travel cost of the tour.
