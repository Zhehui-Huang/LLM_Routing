- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its tour at the depot city (City 0).
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly 7 cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The robot can travel between any two cities, and the travel cost between any two cities is calculated as the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The goal is to find the shortest possible tour that meets the above conditions.
- [Requirement 5] The output should be in the specified format with the list of city indices for the tour starting and ending at City 0, followed by the total travel cost.
