- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its journey at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot needs to visit exactly 7 cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The objective is to find the shortest possible tour that covers these cities.
- [Requirement 4] The travel cost between two cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 5] The tour must be represented as a list of city indices starting and ending at city 0.
- [Requirement 6] The output should also include the total travel cost of the tour.
