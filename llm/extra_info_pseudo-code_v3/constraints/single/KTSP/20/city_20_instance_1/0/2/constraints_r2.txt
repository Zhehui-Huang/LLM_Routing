- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its route at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot needs to visit exactly 7 cities out of the given 20 cities.
- [Requirement 3] The goal is to find the shortest possible route in terms of the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The output should include the tour as a list of city indices, starting and ending at city 0.
- [Requirement 5] The output should include the total travel cost of the tour.
