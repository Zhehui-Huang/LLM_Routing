- [Requirement 1] The robot must visit exactly 10 cities, starting and ending at the depot city (city index 0).
- [Requirement 2] The travel path must form a closed loop, beginning and concluding at the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost is calculated based on the Euclidean distance between cities.
- [Requirement 4] The objective is to find the shortest possible travel path that meets the above conditions.
- [Requirement 5] Output must include the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 6] Output must also include the total travel cost of the tour.
