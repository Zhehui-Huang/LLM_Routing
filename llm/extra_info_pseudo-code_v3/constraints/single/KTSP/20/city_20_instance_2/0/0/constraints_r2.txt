- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its tour at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly 10 out of the 20 provided cities.
- [Requirement 3] The goal is to minimize the total travel cost, calculated as the Euclidean distance between the visited cities.
- [Requirement 4] The tour must be represented as a list of city indices, starting and ending with the depot city.
- [Requirement 5] The output should include both the tour and the total travel cost of the tour.
