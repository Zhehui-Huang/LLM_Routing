- [Requirement 1] The robot starts and ends the journey at depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit a total of 4 cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The route must be the shortest possible tour, minimizing the travel distance.
- [Requirement 4] Travel cost between any two cities is calculated as the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 5] Output the tour as a list of city indices, starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 6] Output the total travel cost of the tour.
- [Requirement 7] Use the General Variable Neighborhood Search (GVNS) algorithm to find the solution.
