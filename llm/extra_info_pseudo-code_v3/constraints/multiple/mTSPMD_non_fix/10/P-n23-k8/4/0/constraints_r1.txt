- [Requirement 1]
Each robot must visit all cities exactly once, with each city being visited by exactly one robot.

- [Requirement 2]
Each tour must start at a designated depot city.

- [Requirement 3]
Robots do not need to return to the depot after completing their tour; they can stop at any city.

- [Requirement 4]
The goal is to minimize the total travel cost for all robots combined.

- [Requirement 5]
The travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between cities.

- [Requirement 6]
There are 23 cities, including 7 depots and 16 other cities.

- [Requirement 7]
All robots start at depot city 0.

- [Requirement 8]
There are 8 robots available to perform the tasks.
