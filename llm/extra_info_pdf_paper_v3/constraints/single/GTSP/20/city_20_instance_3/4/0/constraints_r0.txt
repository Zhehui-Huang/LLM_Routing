- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly one city from each of the 6 city groups.
- [Requirement 3] The robot can travel between any two cities.
- [Requirement 4] The travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between any two cities.
- [Requirement 5] The goal is to find the shortest possible tour that satisfies the first four requirements.
