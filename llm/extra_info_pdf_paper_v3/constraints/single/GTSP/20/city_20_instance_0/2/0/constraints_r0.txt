- [Requirement 1] The robot should start and end its tour at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot needs to visit exactly one city from each of the three groups of cities.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance formula.
- [Requirement 4] The solution should provide both the shortest possible tour (as a list of city indices) and the calculated total travel cost for that tour.
