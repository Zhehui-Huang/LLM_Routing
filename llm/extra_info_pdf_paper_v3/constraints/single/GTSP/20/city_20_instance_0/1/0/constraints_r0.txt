- [Requirement 1] The robot starts and ends its tour at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly one city from each group of cities.
- [Requirement 3] The distance between any two cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance formula.
- [Requirement 4] The objective is to find the shortest possible tour that satisfies the above conditions.
- [Requirement 5] Output should list the tour as a sequence of city indices starting and ending at the depot.
- [Requirement 6] Output should also include the total travel cost of the tour.
