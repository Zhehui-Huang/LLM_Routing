- [Requirement 2] The robot has to visit exactly one city from each city group, where there are a total of 7 city groups.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The output must include the shortest tour sequence starting and ending at city 0 and the total travel cost of this tour.
