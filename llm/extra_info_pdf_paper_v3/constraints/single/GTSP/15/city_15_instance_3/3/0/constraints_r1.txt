- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its tour at the depot city (index 0).
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly one city from each of the 6 city groups.
- [Requirement 3] Each city in the tour must come from a different group.
- [Requirement 4] Travel cost is calculated based on Euclidean distance between cities.
- [Requirement 5] The objective is to find the shortest possible tour given the constraints.
- [Requirement 6] Tour output format: List of city indices starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 7] Total travel cost output format: Numerical value representing the total travel distance.
