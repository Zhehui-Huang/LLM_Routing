- [Requirement 1] There is one robot that starts and ends its journey at depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly one city from each of the specified six city groups.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between cities is calculated as the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The goal is to find the shortest possible tour that satisfies the above constraints.
- [Requirement 5] The output should list the tour starting and ending at city 0 and include the total travel cost of this tour.
