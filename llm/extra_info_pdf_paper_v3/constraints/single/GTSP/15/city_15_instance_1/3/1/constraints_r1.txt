- [Requirement 1] The robot starts its tour at the depot city (city 0) and must end the tour at the same city.
- [Requirement 2] The robot is required to visit exactly one city from each city group. There are four groups, and one city must be selected from each group to visit.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between any two cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance formula.
- [Requirement 4] The solution must find the shortest possible tour that satisfies the given conditions.
- [Requirement 5] The final output must include the ordered tour starting and ending at the city 0 and the total travel cost of this tour.
