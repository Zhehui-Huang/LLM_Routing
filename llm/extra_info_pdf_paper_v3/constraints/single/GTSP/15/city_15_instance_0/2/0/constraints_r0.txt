- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city, which is city index 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly one city from each of the three city groups during the tour.
- [Requirement 3] The total travel path must be the shortest possible, calculated by the Euclidean distance between cities.
- [Requirement 4] The output should list the tour as a series of city indices starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 5] The output should also provide the total travel cost of the tour.
