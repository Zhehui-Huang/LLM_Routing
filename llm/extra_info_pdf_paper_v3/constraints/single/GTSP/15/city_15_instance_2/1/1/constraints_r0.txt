- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its tour at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly one city from each of the five groups of cities during its tour.
- [Requirement 3] Each group contains specific cities: Group 0 contains cities [8, 12, 14], Group 1 contains cities [7, 10, 11], Group 2 contains cities [4, 6, 9], Group 3 contains cities [1, 3, 13], and Group 4 contains cities [2, 5].
- [Requirement 4] The travel cost between cities is calculated based on the Euclidean distance between them.
- [Requirement 5] The output should include the tour as a list of city indices, beginning and ending at city 0.
- [Requirement 6] The output should also include the total travel cost of the tour.
