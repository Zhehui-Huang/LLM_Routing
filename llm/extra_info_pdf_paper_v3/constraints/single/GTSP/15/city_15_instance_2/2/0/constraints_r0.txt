- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its journey at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly one city from each group of cities. There are 5 city groups.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between any two cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] Output the tour as a list of city indices, beginning and ending with city 0.
- [Requirement 5] Output the total travel cost of the tour.
