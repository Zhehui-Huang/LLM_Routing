- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its tour at the depot city (index 0).
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly one city from each of the five city groups during the tour.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance formula.
- [Requirement 4] The solution must find the shortest tour that satisfies these constraints.
- [Requirement 5] Output the tour as a list starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 6] Output the total travel cost of the tour.
