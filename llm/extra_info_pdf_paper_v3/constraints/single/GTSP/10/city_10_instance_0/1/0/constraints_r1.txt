- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end at the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly one city from each of the three city groups.
- [Requirement 3] Total travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between visited cities.
- [Requirement 4] The output must list the city indices in the order they are visited, starting and ending at city 0.
- [Requirement 5] The output must also include the total travel cost of the tour.
