- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly one city from each group of cities during the tour.
- [Requirement 3] Travel cost is calculated using the Euclidean distance between cities.
- [Requirement 4] The house includes 10 cities, each with a specific coordinate.
- [Requirement 5] There are 6 groups of cities, each defining a specific subset of these cities.
- [Requirement 6] The solution must include both the path of the tour and the total travel cost.
- [Requirement 7] The tour output format should be as a list of city indices starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 8] The algorithm should aim to find the shortest possible tour fulfilling all other requirements.
