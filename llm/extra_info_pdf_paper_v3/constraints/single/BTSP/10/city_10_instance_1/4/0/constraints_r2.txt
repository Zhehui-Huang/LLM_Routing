- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its journey at the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 2] Each city must be visited exactly once by the robot, excluding the return to the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The distance between any two cities is calculated using the Euclidean formula.
- [Requirement 4] The objective is to minimize the maximum distance between any two consecutive cities in the robot's tour.
- [Requirement 5] The output must include the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at city 0.
- [Requiredment 6] The output must include the total travel cost of the tour.
- [Requirement 7] The output must include the maximum distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
