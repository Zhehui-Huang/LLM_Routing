- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] Each city must be visited exactly once by the robot, except for the depot city which must be visited twice (at the start and end of the tour).
- [Requirement 3] The goal is to minimize the longest distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
- [Requirement 4] The travel cost between any two cities is calculated as the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 5] The final output should be provided in this format:
  - A list of city indices representing the tour.
  - Total travel cost of the tour.
  - Maximum distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
- [Requirement 6] The tour should consider 20 specified cities with provided coordinates.
