- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit each city exactly once.
- [Requirement 3] Travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between cities.
- [Requirement 4] Minimize the longest distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
- [Requirement 5] Output should include the tour as a list of city indices, total travel cost of the tour, and the maximum distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
