- [Requirement 1]: The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2]: The robot has to visit exactly 8 cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3]: The objective is to find the shortest tour based on the Euclidean distance between cities.
- [Requirement 4]: Compute the total travel cost of the tour and provide the tour route.
