- [The environment consists of 10 cities with specific Cartesian coordinates for each city.]
- [There is one robot that starts and ends its tour at the depot city, which is city 0.]
- [The task is limited to the robot visiting exactly 6 cities, including the depot city.]
- [Travel cost between any two cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance.]
- [The goal is to find the shortest possible tour that visits exactly 6 cities.]
- [Output the tour as a list of city indices, starting and ending at city 0.]
- [Output the total travel cost of the selected tour.]
