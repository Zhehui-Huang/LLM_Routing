- [Requirement 1] The robot needs to start and end the tour at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot has to visit exactly 6 cities from the given set of 10 cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The goal is to find the shortest possible tour satisfying the city visit requirement.
- [Requirement 4] The travel cost between two cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance.
