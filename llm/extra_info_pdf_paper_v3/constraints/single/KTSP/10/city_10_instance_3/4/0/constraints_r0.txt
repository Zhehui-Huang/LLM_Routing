- [The environment includes 10 defined cities with specified coordinates, starting with a depot city at index 0.]
- [There is one robot that starts at the depot city 0 and must return there after completing its tour.]
- [The robot is required to visit exactly 7 cities out of the available 10, inclusive of the starting depot city.]
- [Travel cost between any two cities is determined by their Euclidean distance.]
- [The task is to determine the shortest possible tour visiting exactly 7 cities and returning to the depot city.]
- [The tour results must be presented as a list of city indices starting and ending with the depot city 0 along with the total travel cost of the tour.]
