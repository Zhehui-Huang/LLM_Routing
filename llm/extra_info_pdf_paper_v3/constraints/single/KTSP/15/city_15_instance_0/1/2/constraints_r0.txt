- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its tour at the depot city (index 0).
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly 4 cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The goal is to find the shortest possible tour covering these 4 cities.
- [Requirement 4] The travel cost between any two cities is calculated as the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 5] The output of the solution should be in the format: The tour as a list of city indices and the total travel cost, starting and ending at the depot city 0.
