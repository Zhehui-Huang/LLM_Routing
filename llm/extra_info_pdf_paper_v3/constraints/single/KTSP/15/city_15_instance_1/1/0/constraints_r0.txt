- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly 6 cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between two cities is calculated as the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The goal is to find the shortest possible tour that satisfies the above requirements.
- [Requirement 5] The output format must be: Tour: [list of city indices starting and ending with city 0] and Total travel cost: [numeric value of the total travel cost].
