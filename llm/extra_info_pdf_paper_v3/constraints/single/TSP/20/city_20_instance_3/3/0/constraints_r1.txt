- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit all other cities exactly once.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between any two cities.
- [Requirement 4] The output should be the shortest tour starting and ending at city 0, formatted as a list of city indices.
- [Requirement 5] The output should include the total travel cost of the tour.
