- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit all 20 cities exactly once, excluding the depot city, during the tour.
- [Requirement 3] The distance between any two cities is calculated using the Euclidean formula, and this value represents the travel cost between those cities.
- [Requirement 4] The solution must find the shortest possible tour that adheres to the above requirements.
- [Requirement 5] The output should be presented as a list of city indices starting and ending at the depot city, followed by the total travel cost of the tour.
