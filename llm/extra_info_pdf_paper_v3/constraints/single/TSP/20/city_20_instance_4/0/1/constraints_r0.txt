- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its tour at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit each of the other cities exactly once.
- [Requirement 3] Travel costs between cities are determined by the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] Find the shortest possible tour that satisfies the above conditions.
- [Requirement 5] Output the tour as a list of city indices, starting and ending at city 0.
- [Requirement 6] Output the total travel cost of the tour.
