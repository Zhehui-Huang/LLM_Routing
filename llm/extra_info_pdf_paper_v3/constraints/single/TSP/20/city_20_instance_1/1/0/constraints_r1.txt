- [Requirement 1] The robot starts and ends the tour at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit each city exactly once, excluding the depot city, which it visits twice (start and end).
- [Requirement 3] Travel cost between any two cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance formula.
- [Requirement 4] The task is to find the shortest possible tour that meets the above requirements.
- [Requirement 5] Output the tour as a list of city indices, starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 6] Output the total travel cost of the tour.
