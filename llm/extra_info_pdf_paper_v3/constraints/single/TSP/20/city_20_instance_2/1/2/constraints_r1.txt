- [Requirement 1] The robot must visit each city exactly once, excluding the starting depot city, before returning to the depot city.
- [Requirement 2] The total travel path must start and end at the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 3] Travel cost between two cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance formula.
- [Requirement 4] The output should be formatted as a list of city indices representing the tour, starting and ending at the city 0.
- [Requirement 5] The total travel cost of the tour should also be output.
