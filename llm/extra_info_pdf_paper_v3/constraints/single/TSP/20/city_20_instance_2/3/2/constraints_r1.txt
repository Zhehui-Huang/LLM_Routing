- [Requirement 1]
  The robot must start at the depot city 0.

- [Requirement 2]
  The robot must visit each city exactly once, except the depot city.

- [Requirement 3]
  After visiting all other cities, the robot must return to the depot city.

- [Requirement 4]
  The travel cost is calculated based on the Euclidean distance between cities.

- [Requirement 5]
  Output the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at the depot city.

- [Requirement 6]
  Output the total travel cost of the tour.
