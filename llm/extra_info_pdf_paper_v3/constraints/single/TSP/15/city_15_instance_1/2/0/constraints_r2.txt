- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit each city exactly once, except for the depot city which must be visited twice (as the start and end point).
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between any two cities is calculated as the Euclidean distance between them.
- [Requirement 4] The solution must include finding the shortest possible tour that fulfills these conditions.
- [Requirement 5] Output should include the tour as a list of city indices, starting and ending at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 6] Output should also include the total travel cost of the tour.
