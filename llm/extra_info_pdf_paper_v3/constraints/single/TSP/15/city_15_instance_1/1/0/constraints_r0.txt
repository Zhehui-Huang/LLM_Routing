- [Requirement 1] The robot starts at the depot city (index 0) and must return to the depot city after visiting all other cities.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit each city exactly once, except for the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between cities.
- [Requirement 4] The total travel distance for the robot should be minimized.
- [Requirement 5] Output the tour as a list of city indices, starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 6] Output the total travel cost of the tour.
