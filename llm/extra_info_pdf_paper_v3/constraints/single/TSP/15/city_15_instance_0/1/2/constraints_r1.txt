- [Requirement 1]: The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2]: The robot must visit all cities exactly once, except for the depot city.
- [Requirement 3]: The travel cost between any two cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4]: The solution should output the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 5]: The solution should output the total travel cost of the tour.
