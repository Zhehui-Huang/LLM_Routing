- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its tour at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit all other cities exactly once.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between any two cities is calculated as the Euclidean distance between them.
- [Requirement 4] The tour should be output as a list of city indices, starting and ending at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 5] The output must include the total travel cost of the tour.
