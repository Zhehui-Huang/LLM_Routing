- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] Each city must be visited exactly once by the robot, except for the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between two cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The solution must provide the shortest tour that fulfills these constraints.
- [Requirement 5] The output should comprise a list of city indices depicting the tour sequence and the total travel cost.
