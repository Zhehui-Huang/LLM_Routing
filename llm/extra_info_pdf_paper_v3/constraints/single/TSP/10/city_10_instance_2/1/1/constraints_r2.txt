- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its journey at depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] Each city must be visited exactly once by the robot, except for the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The journey cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between cities, and the goal is to find the shortest possible tour.
- [Requirement 4] The output must include both the tour (as a list of city indices starting and ending at the depot city 0) and the total travel cost of the tour.
