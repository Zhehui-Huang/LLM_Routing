- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its tour at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit each city exactly once, excluding the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between any two cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The solution must find the shortest possible tour that meets the above conditions.
- [Requirement 5] The output of the tour should be in the format of a list of city indices starting and ending at city 0.
- [Requirement 6] The output should also include the total travel cost of the tour.
