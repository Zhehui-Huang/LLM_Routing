- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its journey at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit all other cities exactly once between the start and the end of the tour.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between any two cities is calculated as the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The output should include the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at city 0.
- [Requirement 5] The output should also include the total travel cost of the tour.
