- [Requirement 1] Robots must collectively visit all non-depot cities exactly once and return to the depot city after completing their tours.
- [Requirement 2] Each robot starts and ends its tour at the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] Travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between two cities.
- [Requirement 4] The objective is to determine the shortest possible tours for the robots while minimizing the total travel cost.
- [Requirement 5] Ensure every city, except the depot, is visited exactly once by any of the robots.
- [Requirement 6] Output the tours for each robot as a list of city indices, starting and ending at the depot.
- [Requirement 7] Output the total travel cost for each tour and the overall total travel cost.
