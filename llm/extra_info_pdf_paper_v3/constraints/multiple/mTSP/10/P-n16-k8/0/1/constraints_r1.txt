- [Requirement 1]: Robots must visit all cities exactly once, excluding the depot city, and return to the depot city after completing their tours.
- [Requirement 2]: Each robot starts and ends its tour at the depot.
- [Requirement 3]: The objective is to determine the shortest possible tours for the robots while minimizing the total travel cost.
- [Requirement 4]: The tours for each robot should be outputted as a list of city indices starting and ending at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 5]: The solution should output the travel cost for each robot's tour and the overall total travel cost.
