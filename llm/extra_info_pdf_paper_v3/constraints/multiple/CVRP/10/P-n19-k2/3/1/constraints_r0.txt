- [There are 19 cities, including a depot city (City 0).]
- [Each city, excluding the depot, has a specified demand that must be fulfilled exactly.]
- [There are 2 robots available for delivering goods.]
- [Each robot has a carrying capacity of 160 units.]
- [Each robot starts its route from the depot city (City 0) and must return to the depot city after completing its deliveries.]
- [Travel cost between any two cities is determined by the Euclidean distance.]
- [The objective is to minimize the total travel cost for all tours.]
- [Each robot's total cargo on any given tour must not exceed its carrying capacity.]
