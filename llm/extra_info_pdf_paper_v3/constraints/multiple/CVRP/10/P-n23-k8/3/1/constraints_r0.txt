- [Requirement 1] All robots must start and end their tours at the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 2] All city demands must be fully met by the deliveries made by the robots.
- [Requirement 3] The capacity of each robot (40 units) must not be exceeded.
- [Requirement 4] The objective is to minimize the total travel distance for all robot tours combined.
- [Requirement 5] The travel cost between two cities is calculated based on the Euclidean distance between them.
