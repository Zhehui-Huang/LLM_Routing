- [Requirement 1] The robots must collectively visit all cities exactly once and then return to their respective depots.
- [Requirement 2] Each robot starts and ends at its assigned depot.
- [Requirement 3] The objective is to determine the shortest possible tours for each robot while minimizing the total travel cost for all robots combined.
- [Requirement 4] Output the tours for each robot as a list of city indices, starting and ending at its assigned depot.
- [Requirement 5] Output the total travel cost for each tour and the overall total travel cost.
