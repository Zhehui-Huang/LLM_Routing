- [Requirement 1] Each robot must start and end its tour at its assigned depot.
- [Requirement 2] Each city must be visited exactly once by exactly one of the robots.
- [Requirement 3] The objective is to minimize the total travel cost for all robots combined.
- [Requirement 4] Travel cost between cities is calculated as the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 5] Output for each robot should list the tour starting and ending at its designated depot and include each robot's total travel cost.
- [Requirement 6] Along with individual travel costs, the overall total travel cost for all tours combined should be reported.
