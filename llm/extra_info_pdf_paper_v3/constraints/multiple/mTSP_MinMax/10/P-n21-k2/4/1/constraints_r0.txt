- [Requirement 2] Each robot starts and ends its tour at the depot city.

- [Requirement 4] The travel cost between two cities is calculated as the Euclidean distance between their coordinates.
- [Requirement 5] There are 21 cities with predefined coordinates, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 6] There are 2 robots, and both start from the depot city.
- [Requirement 7] Output the tours for each robot as a list of city indices, starting and ending at depot city 0.
- [Requirement 8] Output the travel cost for each tour and the maximum travel cost among all tours.
