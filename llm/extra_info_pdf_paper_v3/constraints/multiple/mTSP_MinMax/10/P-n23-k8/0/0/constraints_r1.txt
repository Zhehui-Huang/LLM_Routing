- [Requirement 1] Robots must visit all cities exactly once, excluding the depot city as a destination, and return to the depot city after their tours.
- [Requirement 2] Each robot starts and ends its tour at the depot.
- [Requirement 3] The primary objective is to minimize the maximum distance traveled by any single robot.
- [Requirement 4] Output the tours for each robot as a list of city indices, with each tour starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 5] Output the travel cost for each tour.
- [Requirement 6] Output the maximum travel cost among all tours.
