- [Requirement 1] Robots must start and end their tours at the depot city (City 0).
- [Requirement 2] Each city, except for the depot, must be visited exactly once by the robots collectively.
- [Requirement 3] The primary objective is to minimize the maximum distance traveled by any single robot.
- [Requirement 4] Calculate travel cost as the Euclidean distance between two cities.
- [Requirement 5] Output the tours for each robot as a list of city indices starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 6] Output the travel cost for each tour and the maximum travel cost among all tours.
