- [Requirement 2] Each tour must start and end at the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 3] The goal is to minimize the maximum distance traveled by any single robot, ensuring each robot starts and ends its journey at the depot city.
- [Requirement 4] The travel cost is calculated using the Euclidean distance between cities.
- [Requirement 5] Must output the tours for each robot as a list of city indices and include the travel cost for each tour as well as the maximum travel cost among all tours.
