- [Requirement 2] Each robot starts at designated depot city 0 and ends at any city without the need to return to the starting depot.
- [Requirement 3] Optimize tours to minimize the total travel cost for all robots combined, calculated as the Euclidean distance between cities.
- [Requirement 4] Output the tours for each robot as a list of city indices, starting and ending at the designated depot.
- [Requirement 5] Output the total travel cost for each tour and the overall total travel cost.
