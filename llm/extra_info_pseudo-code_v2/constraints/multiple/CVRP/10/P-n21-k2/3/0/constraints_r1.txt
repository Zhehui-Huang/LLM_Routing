- [Requirement 1] The robots must deliver goods to all cities (excluding the depot city) and then return to the depot city after completing their deliveries.
- [Requirement 2] Each city has a specific demand that must be fully met by the robots.
- [Requirement 3] The carrying capacity of each robot must not be exceeded.
- [Requirement 4] Minimize the total travel cost for all tours.
- [Requirement 5] Output the tours for each robot starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 6] Output the total travel cost for each tour.
- [Requirement 7] Output the overall total travel cost.
- [Requirement 8] Travel cost between any two cities is calculated as the Euclidean distance.
