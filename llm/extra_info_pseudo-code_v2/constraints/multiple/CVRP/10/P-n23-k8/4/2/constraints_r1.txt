- [Requirement 1] The robots must start and end their tours at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] Each city's demand must be fully met by the robots' deliveries.
- [Requirement 3] The carrying capacity of each robot must not be exceeded.
- [Requirement 4] Minimize the total travel cost for all tours, calculated as the Euclidean distance between cities.
- [Requirement 5] Output the tours for each robot as a list of city indices, starting and ending at the depot.
- [Requirement 6] Output the total travel cost for each tour and for all tours combined.
