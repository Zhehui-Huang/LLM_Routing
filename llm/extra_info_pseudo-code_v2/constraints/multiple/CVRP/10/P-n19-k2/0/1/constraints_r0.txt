- [Requirement 1] All robots must start and end their route at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] Each city's demand must be fully met by the robots.
- [Requirement 3] The capacity constraints of each robot (160 units) must not be exceeded.
- [Requirement 4] The objective is to minimize the total travel cost for all robot tours.
- [Requirement 5] The travel cost between cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance formula.
