- [Requirement 1] All robots start and end their tour at the depot city, with index 0.
- [Requirement 2] Robots must collectively visit all cities exactly once, excluding the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The objective is to determine the shortest possible tours for the robots while minimizing the total travel cost.
- [Requirement 4] Output format should list the tours for each robot as a list of city indices, beginning and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 5] Output should include the total travel cost for each individual tour and the overall total travel cost.
