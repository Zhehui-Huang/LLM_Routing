- [Requirement 1] Each robot must begin and end its tour at its assigned depot.
- [Requirement 2] Each city must be visited exactly once by the robots collectively.
- [Requirement 3] The objective is to minimize the total travel cost for all robots' tours combined.
- [Requirement 4] Travel costs are calculated using the Euclidean distance between cities.
- [Requirement 5] The tours for each robot as well as the travel costs must be provided as per specified format.
