- [Requirement 1]: The robot must start and end its journey at the depot city (City 0).
- [Requirement 2]: The robot must visit exactly 8 cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3]: The goal is to minimize the total travel cost of the tour, which is calculated based on the Euclidean distance between cities.
- [Requirement 4]: The output must be formatted as a list of city indices indicating the tour starting and ending at the depot city, followed by the total travel cost of the tour.
