- [Requirement 1]: The robot must start and end its tour at the depot city (City 0).
- [Requirement 2]: The robot needs to visit exactly 4 cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3]: The goal is to find the shortest possible tour.
- [Requirement 4]: The robot can travel between any two cities and the travel cost is the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 5]: Output the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 6]: Output the total travel cost of the tour.
