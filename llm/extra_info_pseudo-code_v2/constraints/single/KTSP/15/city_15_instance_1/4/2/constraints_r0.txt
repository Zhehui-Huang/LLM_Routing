- [Requirement 1] The tour must include exactly 6 cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 2] The tour must start and end at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 3] The robot should find the shortest possible tour by minimizing the travel cost where the travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between cities.
- [Requirement 4] The output should be provided as a list of city indices representing the tour and the total travel cost of that tour.
