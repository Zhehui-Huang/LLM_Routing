- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot is required to visit exactly 6 cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The objective is to find the shortest possible tour.
- [Requirement 4] The tour must be returned as a list of city indices, beginning and ending at the depot.
- [Requirement 5] Calculate and return the total travel cost of the tour using Euclidean distance.
