- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its tour at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot is required to visit exactly 10 cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The tour should minimize the travel cost, calculated as the Euclidean distance between cities.
- [Requirement 4] The output must be presented as a list of the visited city indices, starting and ending at city 0.
- [Requirement 5] The output must also include the total travel cost of the tour.
