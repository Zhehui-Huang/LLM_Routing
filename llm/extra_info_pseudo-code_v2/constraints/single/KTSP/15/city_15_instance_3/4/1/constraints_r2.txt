- [Requirement 1] The robot must visit exactly 10 cities, including the starting depot city (City 0).
- [Requirement 2] The tour must start and finish at the depot city (City 0).
- [Requirement 3] The robot can travel between any two cities using the Euclidean distance as the travel cost.
- [Requirement 4] Utilize the General Variable Neighborhood Search (GVNS) algorithm to find the shortest possible tour.
- [Requirement 5] Output the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 6] Output the total travel cost of the tour.
