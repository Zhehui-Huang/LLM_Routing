- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] Exactly 7 cities must be visited in the tour, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The goal is to find the shortest possible tour that visits these 7 cities.
- [Requirement 4] The travel cost is calculated based on the Euclidean distance between any two cities.
- [Requirement 5] The output must be formatted as: Tour: [0, x, y, z, a, b, 0] where x, y, z, a, b are the indices of the cities visited in the tour, and include the total travel cost formatted as: Total travel rate: value.
