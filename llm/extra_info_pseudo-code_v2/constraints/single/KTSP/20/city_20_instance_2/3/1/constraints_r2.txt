- [Requirement 1] The robot starts and ends its tour at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The tour must include exactly 10 cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The objective is to find the shortest possible tour that meets the previous conditions.
- [Requirement 4] The travel cost between two cities is computed using the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 5] Output the tour as a list of city indices, starting and ending at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 6] Output the total travel cost of the tour.
