- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot needs to visit exactly 10 cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The objective is to find the shortest possible tour.
- [Requirement 4] The travel cost between cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 5] The solution output must list the city indices for the tour starting and ending at depot city 0, as well as report the total travel cost of the tour.
