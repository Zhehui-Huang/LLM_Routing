- [Requirement 1] The robot must visit exactly 16 cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 2] The tour must start and end at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost must be calculated as the Euclidean distance between the cities.
- [Requirement 4] The goal is to find the shortest possible tour meeting the above criteria.
- [Requirement 5] Output the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 6] Output the total travel cost of the tour.
