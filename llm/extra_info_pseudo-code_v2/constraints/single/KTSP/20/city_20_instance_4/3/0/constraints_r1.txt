- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end at the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly 16 cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The output should include a tour as a list of city indices, starting and ending at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 4] The output should include the total travel cost of the tour.
- [Requirement 5] The tour must be the shortest possible route that visits 16 cities.
