- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot needs to visit exactly 8 cities out of the given 10.
- [Requirement 3] The objective is to minimize the total travel cost, calculated using the Euclidean distance between cities.
- [Requirement 4] The tour sequence should be returned as a list of city indices.
- [Requirement 5] The output should also include the total travel cost of the tour.
