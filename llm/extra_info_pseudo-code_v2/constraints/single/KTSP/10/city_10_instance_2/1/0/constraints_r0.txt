- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly 6 cities out of the given 10 cities.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between two cities.
- [Requirement 4] The objective is to find the shortest possible tour that respects the above conditions.
- [Requirement 5] The output should include both the tour as a list of city indices and the total travel cost of that tour.
