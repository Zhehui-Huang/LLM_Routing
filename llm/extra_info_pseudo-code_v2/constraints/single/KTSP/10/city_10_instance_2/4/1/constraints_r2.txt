- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot needs to visit exactly 6 cities from the given set of 10 cities.
- [Requirement 3] Tour output must be presented as a list of city indices.
- [Requirement 4] Compute and output the total travel cost of the selected tour.
- [Requirement 5] The travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between two cities.
