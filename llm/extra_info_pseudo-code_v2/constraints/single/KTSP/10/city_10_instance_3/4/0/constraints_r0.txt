- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city (City 0).
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit exactly 7 cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The goal is to find the shortest possible tour.
- [Requirement 4] The travel cost between any two cities is calculated as the Euclidean distance between them.
- [Requirement 5] Output the tour as a list of city indices, starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 6] Output the total travel cost of the tour.
