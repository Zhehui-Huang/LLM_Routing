- [Requirement 1] The robot must visit exactly 7 cities, including the depot city.
- [Requirement 2] All tours must start and end at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost is calculated using the Euclidean distance between cities.
- [Requirement 4] The output format should be a list of city indices, with the tour starting and ending at the depot city 0, and the total travel cost of the tour.
