- [Requirement 1] The robot must visit exactly 5 cities, including the depot city (City 0).
- [Requirement 2] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city (City 0).
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between any two cities is calculated as the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The output must include the tour as a list of city indices, starting and ending at City 0.
- [Requirement 5] The output must include the total travel cost of the tour.
