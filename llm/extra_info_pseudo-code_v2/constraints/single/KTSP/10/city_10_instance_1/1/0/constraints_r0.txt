- [Requirement 1] The robot starts at the depot city (index 0) and must return there.
- [Requirement 2] The robot should visit exactly 5 cities including the depot.
- [Requirement 3] The journey should minimize the total travel cost, calculated as the Euclidean distance between cities.
- [Requirement 4] Output should include the tour as a list of city indices, starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 5] Output should report the total travel cost of the tour.
