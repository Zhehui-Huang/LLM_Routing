- The robot must start and end its journey at the depot city (index 0).
- The robot must visit exactly one city from each specified group of cities.
- The travel cost between cities is calculated as the Euclidean distance.
- The objective is to find the shortest total travel path that satisfies the above requirements.
- The tour output should be in the form of a list of city indices, starting and ending at city 0.
- Alongside the tour, the total travel cost should also be provided.
