- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end at the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit each city exactly once.
- [Requirement 3] The goal is to minimize the longest distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
- [Requirement 4] Output should include the sequence of the tour starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 5] Output should include the total travel cost of the tour.
- [Requirement 6] Output should include the maximum distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
