- [Requirement 1] The robot must visit each city exactly once, starting and ending at the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 2] The objective is to minimize the longest distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
- [Requirement 3] Output should include the tour as a list of city indices, starting and ending at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 4] Output should include the total travel cost of the tour.
- [Requirement 5] Output should include the maximum distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
