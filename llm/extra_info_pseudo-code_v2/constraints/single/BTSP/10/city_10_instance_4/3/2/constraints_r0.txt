- [Requirement 1] The robot must visit each city exactly once, starting and ending at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The goal is to minimize the longest distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
- [Requirement 3] Output must include the tour represented as a list of city indices, starting and ending at city 0.
- [Requirement 4] Output must also include the total travel cost of the tour.
- [Requirement 5] Output must include the maximum distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
