- [Requirement 1] The robot must visit each city exactly once, starting and ending at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The travel cost between any two cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 3] The objective is to minimize the longest (maximum) distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
- [Requirement 4] The output must list the tour starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Department 5] The output must include the total travel cost of the tour.
- [Requirement 6] The output must specify the maximum distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
