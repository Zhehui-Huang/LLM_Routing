- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 2] Each city must be visited exactly once by the robot.
- [Requirement 3] The goal is to minimize the longest distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour (bottleneck distance).
- [Requirement 4] Provide the tour as a list of city indices, starting and ending with the depot city (0).
- [Requirement 5] Output the total distance traveled during the tour.
- [Requirement 6] Output the maximum distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
