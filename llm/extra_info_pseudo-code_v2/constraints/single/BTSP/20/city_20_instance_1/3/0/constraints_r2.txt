- [Requirement 1] The robot must visit each city exactly once, starting and ending at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The goal is to minimize the longest distance between any two consecutive cities in the resultant tour.
- [Requirement 3] Output the tour as a list of city indices with the tour starting and ending at depot city 0.
- [Requirement 4] Output the total travel cost of the tour.
- [Requirement 5] Output the maximum distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
