- [Requirement 1] The robot must visit each city exactly once and return to the depot city 0, forming a complete tour.
- [Requirement 2] The objective is to minimize the longest distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
- [Requirement 3] The output must include the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at the depot city 0, the total travel cost of the tour, and the maximum distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
