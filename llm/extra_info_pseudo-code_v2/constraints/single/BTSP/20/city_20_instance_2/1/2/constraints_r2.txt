- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and finish at the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 2] Each city must be visited exactly once by the robot, forming a tour.
- [Requirement 3] The goal is to minimize the longest distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
- [Requirement 4] The travel cost between any two cities is calculated as the Euclidean distance between these cities.
- [Requirement 5] Output the solution as a tour listing city indices starting and ending at city 0.
- [Requirement 6] Output the total travel cost of the tour.
- [Requirement 7] Output the maximum distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
