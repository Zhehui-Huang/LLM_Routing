- [Requirement 1] The robot should start and end at the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 2] Each city must be visited exactly once.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between any two cities is determined using the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The goal is to minimize the longest distance between any two consecutive cities in the robot's tour.
- [Requirement 5] Output the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at city 0.
- [Requirement 6] Output the total travel cost of the tour.
- [Requirement 7] Output the maximum distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
