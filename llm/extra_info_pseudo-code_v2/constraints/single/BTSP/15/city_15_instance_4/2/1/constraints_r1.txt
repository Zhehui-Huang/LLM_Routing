- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its tour at the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 2] Each city must be visited exactly once by the robot.
- [Requirement 3] Travel cost between two cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance formula.
- [Requirement 4] The tour should minimize the longest distance (maximum distance) between any two consecutive cities.
- [Requirement 5] Output the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 6] Output the total travel cost of the tour.
- [Requirement 7] Output the maximum distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
