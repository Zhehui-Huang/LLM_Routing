- [Requirement 1] The robot must visit each of the 15 cities exactly once, starting and ending at the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 2] The robot's travel cost between any two cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 3] The goal is to minimize the longest distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
- [Requirement 4] The output must be presented as a tour list of city indices starting and ending at the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 5] The output must include the total travel cost of the tour.
- [Requirement 6] The output must specify the maximum distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
