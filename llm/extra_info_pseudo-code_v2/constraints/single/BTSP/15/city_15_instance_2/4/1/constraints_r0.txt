- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit each city exactly once.
- [Requirement 3] The goal is to minimize the longest distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
- [Requirement 4] Calculate the travel cost as the Euclidean distance between two cities.
- [Requirement 5] Implement the heuristic algorithm as described to determine the tour.
- [Requirement 6] Output must include the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 7] Output must include the total travel cost of the tour.
- [Requirement 8] Output must include the maximum distance between any two consecutive cities in the tour.
