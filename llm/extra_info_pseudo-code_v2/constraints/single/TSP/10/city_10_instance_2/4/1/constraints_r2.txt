- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit each of the other cities (1 to 9) exactly once during the tour.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost is calculated as the Euclidean distance between cities.
- [Requirement 4] The task must be solved using a variant of the Lin-Kernighan algorithm.
- [Requirement 5] Output should include the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 6] Output should include the total travel cost of the tour.
