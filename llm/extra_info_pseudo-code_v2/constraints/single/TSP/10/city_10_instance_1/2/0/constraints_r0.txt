- [Requirement 1] The path must start and end at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] Each city must be visited exactly once by the robot, apart from the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between two cities is computed using the Euclidean distance formula.
- [Requirement 4] The robot needs to find the shortest possible tour covering all cities and returning to the depot.
- [Requirement 5] The final output should include the complete tour as a list of indices starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 6] The final output should also include the total travel cost of the tour.
