- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit each city exactly once, except for the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The robot can travel between any two cities, and the cost of travel is determined by the Euclidean distance between them.
- [Requirement 4] The objective is to find the shortest possible tour where all cities are visited.
- [Requirement 5] The tour should be returned as a list of city indices starting and ending at city 0.
- [Requirement 6] The total travel cost of the tour should also be provided.
