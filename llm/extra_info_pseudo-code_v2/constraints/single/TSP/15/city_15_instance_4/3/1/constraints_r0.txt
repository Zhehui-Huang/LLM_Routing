- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its journey at the depot city (city 0).
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit each city exactly once, except the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] Travel cost between any two cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance formula.
- [Requirement 4] The solution should employ a variant of the Lin-Kernighan algorithm for finding the optimal tour.
- [Requirement 5] Output the tour as a list of city indices, starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 6] Output the total travel cost of the complete tour.
