- [Requirement 1] The robot must start at depot city 0 and end its tour back at city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit all other cities exactly once in the tour.
- [Requirement 3] The distance between two cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance formula.
- [Requirement 4] The output must include a list showing the tour as a sequence of city indices, starting and ending at city 0.
- [Requirement 5] The output must also include the total travel cost of the given tour.
