- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end the tour at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot is required to visit each city exactly once, except the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between any two cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The tour should be optimized to find the shortest possible route that satisfies the other conditions.
- [Requirement 5] Output should include both the tour as a list of city indices beginning and ending at the depot, and the total travel cost of this tour.
