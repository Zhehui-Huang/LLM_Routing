- [Requirement 1] The robot must start and end its tour at the depot city, which is city 0.
- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit each city exactly once, excluding the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The total travel cost is calculated based on the Euclidean distance between cities.
- [Requirement 4] The solution should find the shortest possible tour that satisfies the above requirements.
- [Requirement 5] Output should include the tour as a list of city indices starting and ending at city 0 and the total travel cost of the tour.
