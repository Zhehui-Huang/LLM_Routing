- [Requirement 1] The robot starts and ends its tour at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] Each city must be visited exactly once by the robot, except the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The travel cost between any two cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 4] The solution should provide the shortest possible tour that meets the above requirements.
- [Requirement 5] The output of the solution should include both the tour as a list of city indices and the total travel cost of that tour.
