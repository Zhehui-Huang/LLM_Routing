- [Requirement 2] The robot must visit all cities exactly once, except the depot city 0.

- [Requirement 4] The shortest tour route is required.
- [Requirement 5] Output should be formatted as a list of city indices starting and ending at city 0, followed by the total travel cost of the tour.
