- [Requirement 1] The robot must start at the depot city 0.
- [Requirement 2] Each city must be visited exactly once by the robot, except the depot city.
- [Requirement 3] The robot must return to the depot city after visiting all other cities.
- [Requirement 4] Travel cost between two cities is calculated using the Euclidean distance.
- [Requirement 5] The solution must find the shortest possible round trip tour.
- [Requirement 6] Output the tour as a list of city indices, starting and ending at the depot city.
- [Requirement 7] Output the total travel cost of the tour.
